1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Account preferences

Sample Data You Create:

  • Product names and brand names
  • Dates (received dates, due dates)
  • Notes and tags you add
  • Photos you upload
  • Links to products
  • Progress tracking data (filmed, edited, posted status)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Usage Data:

  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Browser type and version
  • IP address (for security purposes only)
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Time and date of access
  • App performance data (crash reports, error logs)

Cookies and Local Storage:

  • Authentication tokens (to keep you logged in)
  • User preferences (dark mode, compact mode settings)
  • Temporary session data

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Provide the Service

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Store your samples and settings in the cloud
  • Send reminder notifications (if enabled)

2.2 Improve the Service

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs

2.3 Communicate With You

  • Send important service updates
  • Respond to support requests
  • Send billing and subscription information
  • Send promotional emails (opt-out available)

2.4 Security and Legal Compliance

  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property

3. How We Store Your Data

3.1 Data Storage

Primary Storage - Supabase:

  • Your account and sample data is stored on Supabase (https://supabase.com)
  • Supabase uses industry-standard encryption
  • Data is stored in secure data centers with redundant backups
  • Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified

Local Storage:

  • Your browser stores authentication tokens and preferences locally
  • This allows the app to work offline temporarily

3.2 Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Encrypted password storage (hashed with bcrypt)
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure API connections

However: No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Lemon Squeezy (Payment Processing):

  • Purpose: Process subscription payments

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law:

  • Court orders or subpoenas

6. Data Retention

Active Accounts:

  • We keep your data as long as your account is active
  • Data is retained to provide the Service

Deleted Accounts:

  • We delete your data within 30 days of account deletion
  • Backups may retain data for up to 90 days for disaster recovery
  • After 90 days, all copies are permanently deleted

Legal Retention:

  • We may retain certain data longer if required by law (e.g., tax records)
  • We may retain anonymized usage statistics indefinitely

  • Parents: If you believe your child created an account, email support@lazybstudios.com

EU Users (GDPR):

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification (correct your data)
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, email support@lazybstudios.com with "GDPR Request" in the subject line.

California Users (CCPA):

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Know if we sell your data (we don't!)
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data sales (not applicable - we don't sell data)

To exercise these rights, email support@lazybstudios.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.
